====== Getting Started ======

===== Goal =====

The goal of Mosmena is not to build industrial sonar. The goal is to help a beginner understand how audio generation, recording, correlation, Android UI state, testing, and documentation all fit together in one repository.

===== First things to understand =====

  * Phone speakers are limited.
  * Phone microphones are limited.
  * Room acoustics are messy.
  * A reflection estimate is always an interpretation of noisy data.

===== First files to read =====

  * `app/src/main/java/com/gedrocht/mosmena/audio/HighFrequencyPulseGenerator.kt`
  * `app/src/main/java/com/gedrocht/mosmena/audio/CorrelationBasedNearestReflectionDistanceEstimator.kt`
