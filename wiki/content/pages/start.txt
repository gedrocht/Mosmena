===== What Mosmena is =====

Mosmena is an Android project that demonstrates a simple pulse-echo ranging workflow:

  - transmit a high-frequency chirp
  - record the response
  - find the direct coupling peak
  - find the first later reflection peak
  - estimate distance from the delay
