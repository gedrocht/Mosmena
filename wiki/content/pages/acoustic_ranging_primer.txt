====== Acoustic Ranging Primer ======

===== The shortest explanation =====

If sound takes longer to come back, the object is farther away.

===== Why the code looks for two peaks =====

The earliest strong peak is usually the phone hearing its own pulse directly.

The next meaningful peak is treated as a reflection from the nearest object.

===== Distance formula =====

Distance equals:

  time delay multiplied by the speed of sound, then divided by two.

The divide-by-two step exists because the pulse travels out to the object and then back again.
